History

For young black girls child prostitution started for them when they were girls were slaved. Many time they would be apart of prostitutionn within slavery. Some slaves were called "Fancy girls and were given resources in exchange for having sex acts with many strangers. Many black woman who fled to the north also had to sell their bodies on order to get proper resources to survive. In that time black woman was also portrayed as these wild sex animals. From violent porn on the media to the strip clubs that were built in what people deem as the "ghetto."

In American child prostitution started for girls in the 1880s and 1900, this was when sexual abuse was very well known at the time. From girls 10-16 years old. After a 60 year battle to make the right law to protect minors the Mann act was passed 1978 to help these minors from sexual exploitation. Throught this one act that was constantly being revolved it led to The Victims of Trafficking and Violence Protection Act and Sex Tourism Prohibition Improvement Act. Which are laws that we go by now.
